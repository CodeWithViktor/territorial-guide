Choosing Your Username

Choosing Your Username

Your username is your identity in the game. Here's what you need to know:

  • It will be displayed in multiplayer matches.
  • Choose something appropriate and memorable.
  • Avoid using personal information.

Username Rules

  • Use letters, numbers, and underscores.
  • Clan names must not exceed 8 characters.

Clans in Territorial.io

What are Clans?

Clans are groups of players who team up to compete together. Joining a clan can enhance your gaming experience and provide a sense of community.

Joining a Clan

  1. Look for clan recruitment messages in the game chat or on community forums.
  2. Contact the clan leader or a recruiter to express your interest.
  3. If accepted, you'll receive instructions on how to add the clan tag to your username.

Creating Your Own Clan

  1. Choose a unique clan name (max 8 characters).
  2. Add the clan tag to your username: [CLAN]Username
  3. Invite other players to join your clan.
  4. Consider creating a Discord server for clan communication.

Clan Benefits

  • Teamwork in multiplayer matches
  • Shared strategies and tips
  • Participation in clan wars and events
  • Social aspect and making new friends

Clan Responsibilities

  • Represent your clan positively
  • Participate in clan activities
  • Help and support fellow clan members
  • Follow clan rules and guidelines

Game Currency (Gold) in Territorial.io

What is Gold?

Gold is the primary in-game currency in Territorial.io. It's used for various purposes and can significantly enhance your gaming experience. Community has also nicknamed it "butter," and it is represented in-game by this emoji: 🧈. Gold is used for various functions in the game, primarily in the lobby. However, it cannot be used to gain any in-game advantage.

Earning Gold

  • Winning matches: For now gold can be earned in only one way and that is by winning matches Earn gold based on your performance and position.
  • In-Game Activities: You can earn gold by participating in multiplayer games. Additionally, other players can transfer gold to you.

How to Spend Gold

  • Viewing your public profile
  • Viewing leaderboards: Clans, Clan members, 1v1 players, and admins
  • Scrolling through the leaderboard
  • Searching for someone on the leaderboard via custom settings
  • Viewing logs for 1v1 reports, Admin elections, Blockchain, and Clan leader elections
  • Sending gold to someone
  • Electing clan leaders
  • Downvoting/upvoting Admins
  • Reporting players
  • Refreshing someone’s profile information
  • Viewing your own public profile
  • Viewing other people's public profiles
  • Viewing/editing charts
  • Uploading or renewing your bio

    • You can identify which actions cost gold by the button's font color. If the font is yellow, pressing it will cost gold.
